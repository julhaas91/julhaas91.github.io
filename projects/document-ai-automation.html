Transforming Manual Document Workflows with Google Cloud Document AI

Python GCP Document AI Docker BigQuery Cloud Storage Cloud Functions

I led the development of intelligent document automation solutions for clients in banking and insurance. Using Google Cloud Document AI, I built systems that automate classification, entity extraction, and scanning of complex financial documents — from loan applications to insurance claims. These solutions achieved up to 80% faster processing compared to traditional manual methods, significantly reducing human error and operational overhead.


The approach replaced slow, error-prone manual workflows with high-accuracy AI pipelines that integrate seamlessly into existing systems via APIs and event-driven Cloud Functions. For highly regulated industries, this meant not just speed but also improved compliance and auditability. The solutions freed up staff to focus on higher-value tasks while accelerating customer-facing processes like loan approvals and claim resolutions — directly improving customer satisfaction.

I was responsible for this project as part of my role as "Head of Machine Learning & GenAI - Google Cloud" at adesso SE.
