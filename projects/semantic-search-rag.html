Semantic Search & RAG for Intelligent Access to Company Knowledge

Python LangChain LangGraph Vertex AI Cloud Run BigQuery Docker Streamlit

I led the design and delivery of AI-powered knowledge systems that fundamentally changed how organizations access their institutional knowledge. By integrating Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with agent-based architectures, I unlocked insights trapped in siloed, unstructured enterprise content — transforming legacy documentation, support tickets, and Confluence spaces into actionable, on-demand intelligence. The solution achieved a 50% improvement in search accuracy compared to legacy keyword-based tools.


The platform empowers service teams, support agents, and business users to ask complex questions and receive accurate, context-aware responses instantly and securely. By combining semantic search with multi-step reasoning agents, the system goes far beyond simple retrieval — it plans, reasons, and integrates both internal and external knowledge sources. The result: faster onboarding, reduced support resolution times, and improved customer satisfaction, all while maintaining enterprise-grade data governance and access control.
