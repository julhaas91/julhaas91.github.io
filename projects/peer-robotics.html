AI-Based Visual Perception for Collaborative Robots (Peer3000)

Python Computer Vision VLMs Robotics Deep Learning

I am developing advanced computer vision and AI capabilities for the Peer3000, a collaborative industrial robot built by Peer Robotics. By applying modern vision-language models (VLMs) and deep learning techniques, I am enabling the robot to perceive its environment with human-like contextual awareness and make proactive, autonomous decisions in real-world manufacturing settings.


Working closely with R&D and manufacturing teams across India, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research, product development, and real-world deployment on the factory floor. The goal is to transform the Peer3000 from a tool that follows instructions into one that truly understands its surroundings — making collaborative robotics smarter, safer, and more adaptive.
