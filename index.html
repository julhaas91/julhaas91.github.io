I am an experienced engineer specializing in artificial intelligence, robotics, machine learning, computer vision, and cloud engineering.

I hold a M.Sc. in electrical engineering with a focus on automation and signal processing. I love to create and build solutions that work in the real world.

My professional career began in biomedical image engineering at Fraunhofer IPT Institute for Production Technology in Aachen, Germany.

Shortly after, I transitioned to IT consulting. In less than 3 years, I advanced from junior to mid-level and senior roles, eventually becoming Head of Machine Learning & GenAI - Google Cloud at the largest German consultancy with over €1.2 billion in revenue and more than 12,000 employees, where I led the strategic alignment of the Google Cloud Competence Center for the domains of machine learning, generative AI, and data engineering.

As a technical lead on multiple projects with budgets of €400k+, I led and mentored 5+ software, data, and machine learning engineers, designing and implementing 10+ production-level AI solutions across 5+ industries (e.g. biomedicine, insurance, banking, lottery, publishers & media companies, energy markets).
